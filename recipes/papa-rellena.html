Papa rellena

Inicio

Description

Papa rellena is a Peruvian dish made of mashed potatoes stuffed with meat, spices, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • 250g ground beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped olives
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes until soft, then mash them.
  2. In a pan, saut√© the onion and bell pepper until soft.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add olives, salt and pepper to the meat mixture.
  5. Form patties with the mashed potatoes, fill with meat mixture and seal.
  6. Coat with egg and breadcrumbs, then fry until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with sauce.
