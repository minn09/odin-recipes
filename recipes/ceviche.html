Description

Ceviche is a typical Peruvian dish that consists of raw fish marinated in lemon or lime juice, accompanied by onion, cilantro, and chili pepper. It's a fresh and light dish, perfect for hot days.

Ingredients

  • 500 g fresh fish (hake, sole, or sea bass)
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 cup of lemon or lime juice
  • 1 limo chili or yellow chili pepper
  • Cilantro to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sweet potato slices and corn (optional)

Steps

  1. Choose fresh fish and cut it into small cubes.
  2. Squeeze lemon or lime juice over the fish.
  3. Add red onion, chopped cilantro, and chili to taste.
  4. Let it marinate for a few minutes before serving.
  5. Serve cold, accompanied by sweet potato slices and corn.
